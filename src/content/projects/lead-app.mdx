## What it is

Lead App is the standalone web application version of Lead Skill. Same persistent-context management operating system, accessible to anyone, not just Claude Code users.

## The problem

Lead Skill works brilliantly inside Claude Code, but most engineering managers don't live in a terminal. The core value, persistent context that compounds your leadership thinking over time, should be available through a web interface.

## How it works

- **Same philosophy.** File-backed persistent context, leadership framework grounding, structured workflows.
- **Web interface.** Accessible from any browser, no CLI required.
- **Broader audience.** Designed for engineering managers who want AI-augmented leadership tools without a developer workflow.

## Why it matters

The pattern proven by Lead Skill is broadly applicable: persistent context + leadership frameworks + structured workflows. A web app makes it accessible to the wider management community.

## Current status

Idea stage. Exploring the right architecture to bring Lead Skill's value to a web experience.
